INSTRUCTIONS FOR RECOMB PROCEEDINGS

This web page contains the guidelines and pertinent information for the final submission of papers for the Second Annual International Conference on Computational Molecular Biology (RECOMB 98).

As indicated below, it is particularly important that you leave sufficient space in the lower left corner of the first column on the first page for ACM to fill in the copyright information. There is a 10-page limit on your paper including everything. You may purchase up to 2 extra pages at $200 per page. The check should be made out to RECOMB 98.

Please submit a camera-ready copy produced on a high-resolution out-put device of your manuscript. The camera-ready format is:

  • 8 1/2" x 11" paper. (Note: many European countries have a longer paper format than the RECOMB standard. Please make all possible efforts to submit your paper in 8 1/2" x 11" format.)

  • 2-column. (This means two vertical columns of type running down the page. For example, Science uses 3-column format. Nature uses 3-column for its news and views and 2-column for articles.)

  • No page numbers.

  • Font: English Times or Times Roman , minimum 9 pt.

  • Margins:
    • Column width: 3.33"
    • 2 column gutter: .33"
    • Left and Right Margin: from edge .75"
    • Top margin: .75"
    • Bottom margin: 1.00"
    • Copyright space on 1st page: lower left column 1"



  • No color figures. The proceedings will be reproduced in black and white and color figures will reproduce at low quality.

A LaTeX style file for this format can be retreived here .

Checklist

  • The ACM copyright form must be signed and returned with your paper. ACM will not publish any paper that is not accompanied by a signed copyright form. In those cases where there is more than one author per paper, the first-named (assumed senior) author only must sign.

  • Sign the first author's name and page number by pencil on the back of each page.
  • Do not use staples, paper clips, or anything that will bend, fold, punch holes through or leave marks on the paper.
  • Your paper and form MUST arrive by January 10, 1998 in order to be included in the proceedings.
  • Please mail your paper and signed copyright form to Pavel Pevzner:
